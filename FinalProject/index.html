Scholarships

In every Fall semester, AU invites applications from fresh entrants for financial grants at the time of applying for admission at AU portal. Based on the information provided by students, Financial Aid Office (FAO) prepares a list of needy/deserving students for financial grants. The scale of all need-based grants will depend on the availability of funds and would be reviewed periodically.

Health Care

Advancing human health through innovative research, education, and care

Health Managment

Air University, a renowned institution committed to academic excellence, places a strong emphasis on the holistic well-being of its students, faculty, and staff. The university takes pride in providing comprehensive healthcare services to ensure the health and welfare of its academic community. With a state-of-the-art health clinic equipped with modern facilities, Air University is dedicated to delivering top-notch medical care, ranging from routine check-ups to specialized health consultations. The university's healthcare professionals are committed to fostering a healthy and supportive environment, addressing both physical and mental well-being. By prioritizing the health of its members, Air University underscores its commitment to nurturing a vibrant and thriving community where individuals can pursue their academic goals in an atmosphere conducive to personal development and success.

Counselling

At Air University, the commitment to the welfare of its students and staff extends beyond academic excellence to encompass a robust healthcare support system. The university takes a proactive approach to health and wellness, providing a range of medical services aimed at maintaining the vitality of its community. The campus health facilities at Air University are equipped with modern resources, offering comprehensive healthcare solutions. Whether it's routine medical check-ups, preventive care, or access to specialized services, the university prioritizes the health of its members. The dedicated healthcare professionals at Air University work tirelessly to create an environment that promotes not only physical well-being but also addresses the mental health needs of the community. Through these efforts, Air University underscores its commitment to creating a supportive and thriving educational ecosystem where individuals can flourish academically and lead healthy, balanced lives.
